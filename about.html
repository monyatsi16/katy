KATLEHO FASHION DESIGN

THIS IS ABOUT OUR COMPANY


History


Our company was born in the so called Sea-point in the Maseru district, lowlands region, Lesotho. We have grown in this environment and we took an upgrade to hire a renting house at the middle of the maseru city in order to reach a variety of customers because of the CBD. Highly oriented towards new challenges,Katleho Fashion Design's mission is to provide innovative solutions and creativity to help customers to wear with a style. The strong relationship we have with top-tier fashion design companies is testimonial to the high level of skill and quality of services offered to our clients.


Objectives


One of the keys to Katleho Fashion Design success is our ability to work together as a team, and support each other in finding the best solutions for different designs. Sharing know-how and experience enables us to maximize the performance of our products and services, adding value to clients.To face every day challenges knowledge and expertise are essential, but not enough if you are not driven by passion. This passion is what we share in our team, and what keeps us striving for excellence in everything we do.


Aims


To deliver excellent services and design innovative products. Our team skills and competences are of crucial importance. To pursue a continuous training program both internally by sharing technical knowledge and organizing specific training sessions, and also externally through different certification programs. To sponsor several PhD programs, as we truly want the best preparation for our team, turned into first-class service for our customers.


Organisational structure


When we select our new employees, we seek passionate and talented people, with excellent hard and soft skills. Essentially we look for people who are creative, constructive and ready to experiment new things. Our team is characterized by three levels of experience: Senior, Experienced and Junior designers.
